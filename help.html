Help Center

Everything you need to know about using Luminaries of Islam

🧭 Navigation Guide

Main Sections

  • Home — Welcome screen with voice search
  • Scientists — Browse all 12 scholars with filter options
  • Timeline — Visual chronological journey through centuries
  • Challenges — Quiz to test your knowledge and earn badges

How to Navigate

Use the top navigation bar to jump between sections. On mobile, tap the ☰ menu icon to reveal navigation links.

🎓 Level System

Every scientist's story is told at three levels:

  • 🟢 Newb — Simple explanations with fun analogies. Perfect for beginners!
  • 🔵 Explorer — Technical details with proper vocabulary. For curious minds!
  • 🟣 Developer — Deep historical and scientific analysis. For serious scholars!

Switch levels using the buttons in the navigation bar or inside each scientist's detail page.

🔊 Text-to-Speech (TTS)

How to Use Read Aloud

  • Look for the 🔊 speaker icon on cards, in detail views, and in the quiz
  • Click it to have the app read the content aloud
  • A control bar will appear at the bottom with play/pause, stop, and speed controls

Speed Control

  • 🐢 Slow — Easier to follow for younger learners
  • 🚶 Normal — Default reading speed
  • 🐇 Fast — For quick review

Browser Support

TTS works in Chrome, Edge, Safari, and Firefox. If you don't hear anything, check your device volume.

🎙️ Speech-to-Text (STT)

How to Use Voice Search

  • Click the microphone 🎤 button on the home search bar
  • Say a scientist's name: "Ibn al-Haytham" or "Al-Khwarizmi"
  • The app will search and may open their profile automatically

Voice Quiz Answers

  • In the quiz section, click the microphone button
  • Say the answer option out loud
  • The app will try to match your answer

Browser Support

STT works best in Chrome and Edge. If the microphone button is not visible, your browser may not support voice recognition. All features remain usable via click/type.

Privacy

No voice data is ever stored or sent. Voice recognition runs locally in your browser. We respect your privacy completely.

🏆 Quiz & Badges

  • The quiz presents 8 randomized questions about the scientists
  • Click an option or use voice to answer
  • Earn badges based on your score:
  • 🌱 Young Scholar — Keep learning!
  • 📚 Knowledge Seeker — Great progress!
  • 🌟 Golden Age Expert — Outstanding mastery!

You can restart the quiz anytime for a fresh set of questions.

🔧 Troubleshooting

No sound from TTS?

Check your device volume. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended). Ensure no other speech is playing.

Microphone not working?

Make sure you've granted microphone permission when prompted. Check browser settings → Site Permissions → Microphone.

Cards not showing?

Check if a filter is active. Click "All" in the filter bar to show all scientists.

App looks broken?

Ensure you have all files in the correct folder structure. Open index.html directly in a modern browser.

📚 Learning Path

Recommended learning journey:

  1. Start at 🟢 Newb level and browse all scientist cards
  2. Read fun facts and listen to TTS summaries
  3. Take the quiz at Newb level
  4. Switch to 🔵 Explorer and re-read the detailed bios
  5. Explore the timeline to see connections between scholars
  6. Take the quiz again at Explorer level
  7. Finally, dive into 🟣 Developer level for full depth
  8. Aim for the 🌟 Golden Age Expert badge!
